The 1975

Penyanyi & Penulis Lagu

Group Name: The 1975

Group Members: Matthew Healy (Vokal, guitar), Adam Hann (Vokal, guitar), George Daniel (Drump), Ross McDonald (Bass)

Formed on Date: 2 September 2013

Origin: Wilmslow, Cheshire England

Genre: POP Rock, Indie POP, Indie Rock

Website: the 1975

Journey

The group initially played cover songs until they began writing and making music together when they were around 15. After Hann encouraged the members to form a band, they spent their early years playing punk covers at local clubs. Healy was originally the drummer, but took over vocals after the previous singer left to start a new band. George Daniel was recruited as the new drummer to complete the group.
